Stroke, Trauma, Cardio & More!

EB Medicine has CME resources suited to everyone. We offer regular doses of pediatric, trauma, stroke, ethics, pharmacology, cardiac, pain management, and other kinds of CME credit. You can access all these via the app or our mobile-friendly website, including CME testing with tracking and on-demand certificates.

What's Included with an Annual Subscription

You'll get 12 new CME courses, published monthly, PLUS access to previously published courses for up to three years back! That’s up to 192 CME credits per publication. Each course includes a summary version to save you time; a flow chart that takes you from presentation to disposition; and risk management pitfalls.

Designed to help you with the decision-making process, for each issue we produce, we create Clinical Pathways. These charts provide criteria to help determine the best treatment options given symptoms, resources, and time.
